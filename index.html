This private league is conducted on the My11Circle app. It consists of all 74 matches of the IPL 2026 season. This document serves as the official agreement for all participating members.

  • Total Matches: 74
  • Entry Fee per Match: ₹20
  • Platform: My11Circle
  • Total Advance Deposit: ₹1,480 per member

4. OPERATIONAL RULES & CONTINGENCIES

  • Advance Payment: Every member must pay their full advance (₹1,480) before Match 1 starts. No "pay-as-you-go" allowed.
  • Volunteer Role: A designated volunteer will manage the accounts. They will take screenshots of the Top 5 rankings after every match to ensure transparency.
  • App Contingency: If My11Circle does not generate a season-long leaderboard, the 10% Reserve Fund will be merged into the Daily Prize Pool, increasing the daily winnings for all 74 matches.
  • Disputes: The rankings shown on the My11Circle app at the time of "Match Completed" status will be final.
