1. LEAGUE OVERVIEW & STRUCTURE

This private league is conducted on the My11Circle app. It consists of all 74 matches of the IPL 2026 season. This document serves as the official agreement for all participating members.

  • Total Matches: 74
  • Entry Fee per Match: ₹20
  • Platform: My11Circle
  • Total Advance Deposit: ₹1,480 per member

  • Advance Payment: Every member must pay full advance (₹1,480) before Match 1. No "pay-as-you-go".

  • App Contingency: If no season leaderboard exists, the 10% Reserve Fund merges into the Daily Prize Pool.
